## Preply vs. Alternatives: Honest Comparison

**italki** — Similar marketplace model. Community tutors start at $8, professional teachers at $15–$40. Slightly lower average prices but fewer built-in scheduling tools and no automatic matching algorithm.

**Cambly** — Fixed-rate conversation practice ($10.80–$15.60/hour). No lesson planning or structured curriculum; best for casual speaking only. No trial lesson needed.

**VIPKid** — Primarily serves young Chinese learners. Structured curriculum and fixed schedules. Not suitable for adult professionals or flexible timing.

**Verbling** — Smaller tutor pool, higher average rates ($25–$60). Strong on group classes and corporate packages but less flexible for individual adult learners.

Preply stands out for its large tutor database (over 20,000 English instructors) and automated matching, while italki often wins on pure price for budget-conscious users.
