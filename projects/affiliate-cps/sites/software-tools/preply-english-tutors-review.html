Why Online English Tutoring Matters in 2026

Global business, travel, and remote work continue to demand strong English skills. Traditional classroom courses often move too slowly or focus on grammar drills instead of real conversation. Online 1-on-1 tutoring solves this by letting learners practice speaking immediately with a dedicated tutor who adjusts to their level, goals, and schedule. Whether you need business English for promotions, IELTS preparation, or casual fluency for travel, personalized online lessons deliver faster results than self-study apps alone.

What Is Preply and How Does It Work?

Preply is an online language-learning marketplace that matches students with independent tutors worldwide. Founded in 2012 and headquartered in Europe, the platform specializes in English but also offers 50+ other languages. The process is straightforward: create a free account, complete a short questionnaire about your goals and preferred schedule, and Preply's algorithm suggests 5–10 tutors. You browse profiles that include video introductions, student reviews, lesson statistics, and hourly rates. Once you find a match, book a trial lesson directly through the calendar. After the lesson, you can continue with the same tutor or switch at any time—no long-term contracts required.

Preply Pricing Breakdown (2026)

Preply does not charge a monthly subscription. You pay only for the lessons you book. Hourly rates are set by tutors and typically range from:
  • Entry-level tutors (non-native, conversational focus): $10–$18 per hour
  • Experienced tutors (TEFL-certified, 5+ years experience): $19–$28 per hour
  • Premium native speakers (business specialists, exam coaches): $29–$45 per hour
Platform commission is built into the displayed price; students see the final amount upfront. Most learners spend $80–$200 per month for 4–8 lessons. Payment methods include credit card, PayPal, and Apple Pay. Unused lesson credits can be rescheduled or refunded within Preply's policy window.

Who Should Use Preply?

Preply works best for:
  • Busy professionals needing business English or interview practice around irregular schedules
  • Students preparing for IELTS, TOEFL, or Cambridge exams who want targeted feedback
  • Intermediate learners who already know grammar but need speaking confidence
  • Parents seeking qualified tutors for children aged 7–17
  • Anyone who values the ability to switch tutors quickly without penalties
It is less ideal for absolute beginners on a very tight budget or those seeking only unstructured chat.
